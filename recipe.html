Chocolate cake

"This chocolate cake is a clasic recipe. This version of recipe is fast and quick and your kids will love it."

Dificulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

    Black chocolate: 200g

    Sugar: 1500g

    Butter: 200g

    Eggs: 4

    Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Mel the chocolate and butter togheter

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

Mix everyting to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven and do this do that to get a very long text.
